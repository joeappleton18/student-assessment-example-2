Welcome to the MAXIMUS ThoughtCloud

What is ThoughtCloud?

ThoughtCloud is a collborative sharing platform for staff from across MAXIMUS UK to share their ideas on how we can improve our business.

How does it work?

You can submit your own ideas, review and vote on other ideas and respond to our challenges. All ideas and responses are reviewed by the Operational Excellence Panel who will select ideas to progress. If your idea is selected, you could be in for a reward!

Who can submit ideas?

Anyone that works here! All staff are encouraged to put ideas forward, either as individuals or as a team.

There are no bad ideas!

If you have an idea that you think will help, no matter the scale or if you think it may be a little odd, we want to hear from you. Some ideas may not work out, but then you don't get anywhere by not trying!

Contribute

Everyone in MAXIMUS will have ideas from the best way to engage partcipants to how we manage our people. If you think that your idea can help us improve, we want to hear from you!

Get rewarded

Let us know your idea and we'll review everything that comes through. Any ideas which are selected could be in line for a reward.

Collaborate

Why not get together with your colleagues over a cuppa in MAXTime to work on our challenges and see what you can brew up!

Be creative

Challenges are focussed on current and future business issues and we want to hear innovative solutions from the people that know our business best - you.

Change

Have a look at your colleagues' ideas and vote up the ones which you like the sound of!

Make a difference

We'll take into account the popularity of an idea when deciding which ones to look into further, although well-liked suggestions for 52 weeks paid holiday a year probably still won't get picked - sorry!
